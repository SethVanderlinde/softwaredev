Preperation

  1. Prep and smoke the ham
  2. Preheat the smoker to 200°F add hard apple cider, cover tight with foil and increase the temp ot 325°F. Cook until internal temp is at 200-204°F
  3. Make Glaze:
  4. While Ham cooks prep the glaze by mixing all the ingredients in a saucepan and simmering for 5 mins
  5. Shred and Mix:
  6. Once cooked shred the ham using protective gloves and mix in some of the cooking liquids for added moisture
  7. Apply glaze and serve:
  8. Drizzle the shredded ham with the prepared glaze and serve hot
